About Me

Hi there, I’m Fatima! and I’m a Multimedia Design and Development (MMDD) student at Humber College, North Campus. Being enrolled in this program is a self-fulfilled dream of my younger self ten years ago. I had a passion for arts and design way back in high school, but I didn’t have the courage to pursue it earlier because of perceived career instability in the Philippines after I graduated. Thus, making it into this program and pursuing my studies abroad is really a big leap of faith, involving a major career shift in my late twenties. Nevertheless, I am grateful to have been given this opportunity to realize my dream after a decade. Despite the lack of a solid foundation in design, I see this as an opportunity to challenge myself to learn and harness my skills.


At this time, I am still figuring out and exploring what could be my career niche after I graduate from my program, as my interests lean towards becoming an Illustrator, Web Designer, or Video Editor. I’m also a travel enthusiast and I love to explore new places and different cultures that help me enrich my learning experiences. While I was still in the Philippines sometime in 2023, I had the wonderful time to engage in biking activity, allowing me to explore our small town and visit nearby beach shorelines.
